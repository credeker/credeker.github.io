Medusa Myths - Twine Interactive Narrative

Proof of concept Twine created with Liz Langridge and Megan Mitchell at the 2018 Digital Humanities Summer Institute at the University of Victoria, BC. This Twine lets readers explore the multiple versions of the Greek Medusa Myth.

Digital Story Dice

CodePen app created to inspire the creation of a new fairy tale. Roll the digital dice and create a story with the elements proposed.

Biblibot

A Twitterbot that reminds students to do the reading for their English classes. Biblibot also tweets out book recommendations and pithy quotes about the pleasures of reading. This Twitterbot acted as a class model for an advanced technical communication course--see Teaching: Engl 421 for more student-created Twitterbots.
